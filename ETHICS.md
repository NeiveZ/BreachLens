# Ethics

BreachLens is for defensive security, authorized assessments and lab work.

Allowed use:

- Checking your own e-mail, domains and passwords.
- Checking client-owned assets with written authorization.
- Scanning local files that you are allowed to review.
- Validating whether exposed credentials require rotation.

Disallowed use:

- Scraping dark-web markets or credential forums.
- Downloading or redistributing leaked credential databases.
- Using found credentials to access systems.
- Printing, sharing or storing plaintext passwords in reports.
- Bypassing API access controls or paid service limits.

The tool masks secrets by design and clearly marks coverage limitations.
